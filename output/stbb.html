STBB Direct
A mobile software application for property and conveyancing
client
Established in 1900, STBB is a firm of approximately 90 business-minded lawyers practicing from 12 branches throughout South Africa primarily in conveyancing.
project description
STBB Direct was designed for buyers, sellers and property agents to assist them is tracking deals, allow financial calculations and produce and refine documentation.
Additionally the system includes secure document transfer and traking between parties and long term secure document storage.
my role
In conjunction with a team from Polymorph I designed and developed the backend and front end solutions. I led the development team and completed all architecture and security designs.
technology
Backend and storage services are provided by google firebase using realtime, firecloud and firebase storage. Front end client are provided for Android iOS and browser using Ionic wrapped with Apache Cordova.
current status
The software is in constant use by several thousand clients and agents throughout South Africa and the world.
