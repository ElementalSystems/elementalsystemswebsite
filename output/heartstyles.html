Heartstyles
A personal development tool for the digital age
client
Heartstyles is a large company focussed on development of employees at both a personal and leadership level. They currently train and develops thousands of people a year using their well establish methodology.
project description
The heartstyles application is the first attempt to expand the Heartstyles methodology into the digital realm. The software guides users through the heartstyles philosophy and has large educational modules.
Additionally it presents and explains the results of the surveys taken by the user as graphs and custom created video content.
The application encourages cultivation of the techniques and ideas via a private journal, daily ideas for reflection and the ongoing monitoring of actions to change your behaviour.
my role
I worked with polymorph on the development of the design and business ideas. I designed the software architecture and led the development team from the initial ideas until the second broad rollout of the software.
technology
Backend services and secure storage are provided by AWS. The mobile application software is developed in Flutter.
current status
The software is in daily use by hundredds of individuals. Development is ongoing in both scope and technology.
