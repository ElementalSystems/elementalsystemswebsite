IHG Planner
A human resources manangement tool
client
The International Hotels Group runs over 6000 hotels in almost every territory and timezone. They include major hotel brands including InterContinental, Crowne Plaza and Holiday Inn.
project description
The application software is used to analyse the operations and efficiency of the human resources in the hotels.
It includes long term resource planning and tools to reflect on past uses of available resource as well as day to day shift planning and resource allocation.
Two separate interacting tools exist for different levels of the business, from planning to management to everyday operations.
my role
I worked with Sudo to create and support the tools. I am the designer, architect and implementor of the front end application software.
technology
Backend services are provided by Google Firebase and Google Cloud Platform.
All services are coded in typescript.
The front end is developed for any browser using Angular and primeNG for visual effects.
current status
The software is in daily use hundreds of hotels consisting of thousands of active daily users. The project is currently rolling out to the remaining territories. New development is ongoing including a full clocking system for service employees and multi-lingual operations.
